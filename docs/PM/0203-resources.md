## Resource Constraints & Risks

### Constraints
1. **Small Team:** 3-4 people for 6-month project
2. **Part-time Resources:** BA, PM, QA are part-time
3. **Full-stack Developer:** Backend & frontend may be same person
4. **Budget:** Open-source, limited budget for tools/services

### Risks
1. **Resource Overload:** Full-stack developer may be overloaded
2. **Timeline Pressure:** 6 months for full project may be tight
3. **Skill Gaps:** Team may lack certain skills (DevOps, Security)
4. **Availability:** Part-time resources may have conflicting priorities

### Mitigation Strategies
1. **Resource Overload:** Prioritize MVP features, defer nice-to-have features
2. **Timeline Pressure:** Use agile methodology, adjust scope if needed
3. **Skill Gaps:** Use external consultants for specialized tasks
4. **Availability:** Clear communication, regular check-ins, buffer time
